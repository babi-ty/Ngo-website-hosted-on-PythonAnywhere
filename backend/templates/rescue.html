WHAT TO DO WHILE WAITING FOR RESCUE

  • Stay with the animal if safe to do so, keeping a safe distance if the animal appears aggressive or frightened.
  • Keep the animal calm and avoid loud noises or sudden movements that might cause additional stress.
  • If safe, provide water in a shallow container, but do NOT force-feed the animal.
  • Take clear photos of the animal and exact location to share with our rescue team.
  • Mark the precise GPS location and share nearby landmarks for easy navigation.
  • If the animal is on the road, try to alert passing traffic to prevent further accidents.
  • Do NOT attempt to move severely injured animals unless they're in immediate danger.
  • For snake rescues, maintain safe distance and note the snake's appearance and location.
  • Keep your phone accessible to receive calls from our rescue coordinator.
