On-Site Volunteering Activities

Animal Care Tasks

  • Abandoned Pets SectionSection disinfection, bathing, dog walking
  • Geriatric DogsSection disinfection, gentle bathing
  • Mange DogsMedicated baths (20-30 min), oil applications, section disinfection
  • Paraplegic DogsHand feeding, therapy support, section maintenance
  • Puppy/Kitten ShelterHand feeding, deworming, cleaning, de-ticking

Other Sections

  • Cattle SectionSection disinfection, bathing, feeding
  • Donkey & Horse StablesSection disinfection, bathing, walking
  • AviaryDisinfection, sawdust replacement, hand feeding, cage washing
  • General SectionsCasualty, treatment areas, ABC sections - daily disinfection

Note: Section disinfection involves dry cleaning, high-pressure washing, medicated wash, or blow torching as required.
